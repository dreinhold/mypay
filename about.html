Hourly Pay

Why does this exist

I just created this one day after working too many hours. Being salary has it's positives and no way would I want to punch a clock. But some days it can get bad.

What is this

It makes some serious assumptions, that the yeary salary of a person directly relates to an hourly rate based on a 40 hour work week. It also assumes any time over 40 hours per week is normally paid at x1.5 time.

The page takes these two assumptions and calculates a persons hourly rate, then takes all the hours worked over 40 and dispalys the overtime you would have recived if you were an hourly working making the listed hourly rate.
